## 🎯 What We Do

SeekAlpha lets anyone with a Solana wallet:
1. Browse prediction markets (crypto, sports, politics, memes, culture)
2. Place a bet (YES or NO) using SOL cryptocurrency
3. Win SOL if their prediction is correct

**How we make money:** 3% fee on every winning payout. If a market has $1,000 in total bets and resolves, we earn $30 in fees automatically via the smart contract. No manual collection needed — it's coded into the blockchain.

**Example:** 
- A market asks "Will BTC hit $100K by April?"
- 100 people bet YES totaling 50 SOL (~$7,500)
- 50 people bet NO totaling 30 SOL (~$4,500)
- Total pool: 80 SOL (~$12,000)
- Market resolves YES → YES bettors split the 80 SOL pool proportionally
- Platform automatically takes 3% = 2.4 SOL (~$360) in fees
