## How It Works (Step by Step)

1. **Go to seekalpha.bet**
2. **Connect your Phantom wallet** (install from phantom.app if needed)
3. **Get devnet SOL** — go to faucet.solana.com, paste your wallet address, request airdrop (it's free test money)
4. **Switch Phantom to devnet** — Settings → Developer Settings → Change Network → Devnet
5. **Browse markets** — filter by category, check flash markets for quick bets
6. **Place a prediction** — click a market, choose YES or NO, enter amount, sign the transaction
7. **Track your bets** — check Portfolio page for all your positions
8. **Claim winnings** — when a market resolves, if you won, click "Claim" on your Portfolio

## FAQ — Ready-to-Use Answers

### Getting Started

**Q: What is this?**
A: SeekAlpha is a prediction market on Solana! You bet YES or NO on real-world outcomes — crypto, sports, politics, AI. If you're right, you win. It's like Polymarket but on Solana 🔮

**Q: How do I start?**
A: Easy! Go to seekalpha.bet → connect Phantom wallet → get free devnet SOL from faucet.solana.com → start predicting! Takes about 2 minutes to set up.

**Q: What wallet do I need?**
A: Phantom wallet — get it at phantom.app. Works as a browser extension or mobile app.

**Q: Is it free to try?**
A: 100% free! We're on devnet right now so you're using test SOL, not real money. Zero risk to try it out.

**Q: How do I get devnet SOL?**
A: Go to faucet.solana.com, paste your Phantom wallet address, and hit "Request Airdrop." You'll get free devnet SOL in seconds.

**Q: How do I switch Phantom to devnet?**
A: Open Phantom → Settings (gear icon) → Developer Settings → Change Network → select "Devnet." Now your wallet talks to the test network.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Nope! No account, no email, no KYC. Your Solana wallet IS your account. Connect and go.

### How It Works

**Q: How does betting work?**
A: Pick a market → choose YES or NO → enter your SOL amount → sign the transaction with your wallet. When the market resolves, winners claim from the pool (minus 3% fee). Your payout depends on the YES/NO pool ratio.

**Q: What's the fee?**
A: 3% on winnings only. If you lose, no extra fee. That's one of the lowest in prediction markets — Kalshi charges around 10%.

**Q: How are payouts calculated?**
A: Proportional to the pools. Example: YES pool has 10 SOL, NO pool has 5 SOL. If YES wins, all YES bettors split the full 15 SOL pool (minus 3% fee) based on their share.

**Q: Who resolves markets?**
A: The admin team resolves markets based on real-world outcomes. Oracle integration (automated resolution) is on the roadmap.

**Q: Can I change my bet?**
A: You can add more to your existing position (same side), but you can't switch from YES to NO or vice versa on the same market.

**Q: What are Flash Markets?**
A: Quick-resolution markets — 1 hour or 24 hours. They have live countdown timers and are perfect for fast-paced betting. Look for the ⚡ icon!
