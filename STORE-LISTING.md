## Full Description

SeekAlpha is a prediction market platform built on Solana where you can stake your conviction on real-world outcomes. Whether it's crypto price moves, protocol launches, governance votes, or cultural events — SeekAlpha lets you put skin in the game with transparent, on-chain markets.

### How It Works
Browse live markets, pick YES or NO, and place your prediction. When the market resolves, correct predictors split the pool. It's that simple.

### Features

**⚡ Flash Markets**
Short-duration markets that resolve in minutes to hours. Perfect for fast-moving events like price targets, announcement outcomes, and breaking news.

**📊 Binary Predictions**
Clean YES/NO markets with transparent odds. No complex order books — just pick a side and predict.

**🔗 On-Chain Transparency**
Every market, every prediction, every resolution lives on Solana. Fully verifiable. No hidden house edge, no opaque settlement.

**💰 Low Fees**
Just 3% on winnings. No deposit fees, no withdrawal fees, no hidden costs.

**🏆 Leaderboard**
Compete against other predictors. Top forecasters rise to the top based on accuracy, volume, and consistency.

**🔥 Streaks & Badges**
Build prediction streaks and earn badges for milestones. Show off your forecasting track record.

### Getting Started
1. Connect your Solana wallet
2. Browse active markets or search by category
3. Select YES or NO and choose your stake amount
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
5. Wait for resolution and claim your winnings

### Built on Solana
SeekAlpha leverages Solana's speed and low transaction costs to deliver instant market creation, real-time odds updates, and near-zero gas fees. Markets settle on-chain with verifiable resolution sources.

### Why Prediction Markets?
Prediction markets aggregate collective intelligence. They've been shown to outperform polls, pundits, and models at forecasting real-world events. SeekAlpha makes participating in this wisdom-of-crowds mechanism accessible to everyone.
